N1/N2/N3/N5 Series

N1/N3 Series

N2/N5 Series

Neutrone® Top Entry (Light/Medium/Heavy Duty) Mixers, N Series are designed to efficiently handle a range of mixing tasks, from light to medium viscosity materials, with capabilities for both high flow and high shear agitation. These mixers are ideal for small batch operations as well as continuous processes, providing versatility across different industrial applications.

Key Features & Benefits:

Direct Drive (N1 & N3 Series)

Offer direct drive systems that provide reliable performance and are designed for ease of use in applications where efficient and high-speed mixing is required.

Gear Reduction (N2 & N5 Series)

Designed to deliver higher torque, making them suitable for applications requiring lower speeds with more force. This setup ensures consistent and reliable mixing, particularly for higher viscosity materials.

Low Operating Costs

Designed for cost-effective operation, with a focus on minimizing maintenance and energy consumption while maximizing performance.

Durability and Long Life

Built for longevity and engineered to withstand rigorous industrial environments, ensuring long-term, trouble-free operation.

Neutrone® Top Entry (Light/Medium/Heavy Duty) Mixers, N Series deliver consistent, high-quality mixing results, whether in batch or continuous processing environments, at a competitive price point.
