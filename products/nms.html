NMS Series

Neutrone® In-Liner Static Mixers (NMS/NMSR Series) is a high-performance mixing solution designed to efficiently blend liquids, gases, and solids without the need for moving parts. These mixers offer a wide range of customization options and are available in various materials and diameters, making them ideal for a broad spectrum of industrial applications. Here's an overview of its key features and benefits:

Key Features of Neutrone® In-Line Static Mixers, (NMS/NMSR Series):

Custom Designs

Available in customized designs to meet specific process requirements. Whether you need a specialized material or a specific diameter, the mixers can be tailored to suit your needs.

Wide Range of Diameters

Variety of sizes, ranging from 1/2 inch to 72 inches in diameter. This range allows for flexibility in both small-scale and large-scale applications, ensuring that you can find the right mixer for your system

Advanced Design Elements

Incorporate advanced design elements, including fixed and removable mixing elements. These elements ensure optimal flow division and radial mixing, providing consistent and uniform results.

The fixed elements are designed for continuous, maintenance-free operation, while the removable elements can be taken out for cleaning or modifications, offering operational flexibility.

Radial Mixing and Flow Division

Unique radial mixing and flow division design is key to the performance of the Neutrone® In-Line Static Mixers. This ensures that the materials (liquid, solid, or gas) are evenly distributed and mixed in a uniform manner throughout the system, which is critical for processes requiring high precision.

Efficient Mixing Without Moving Parts

Do not use any moving parts, which means there is no need for motors or complex mechanical components. This results in lower energy consumption, reduced maintenance costs, and fewer points of failure compared to traditional mixers.

Material Options

Available in a variety of materials to handle different fluids and environments. Materials can include stainless steel, plastic, carbon steel, hastelloy, titanium, and other alloys. This ensures compatibility with highly corrosive, abrasive, or temperature-sensitive materials.

No External Energy Source Required

Rely on the flow energy of the process fluid to achieve mixing. As a result, they do not require external power sources (such as motors or pumps), making them a highly energy-efficient option for many applications.

Benefits of Neutrone® In-Line Static Mixers:

  • Low Maintenance: Since there are no moving parts, there’s minimal wear and tear, resulting in lower maintenance costs and longer service life.
  • High Efficiency: These mixers provide consistent, high-efficiency mixing with minimal pressure drop. The radial mixing action ensures optimal blending of fluids and solids in a wide range of applications.
  • Compact and Robust: Neutrone In-Line Static Mixers are designed to fit into tight spaces, making them ideal for installations where other mixing solutions might be too bulky or impractical.
  • Energy Saving: With no need for external power, static mixers operate purely based on the flow energy of the process stream, leading to reduced operating costs and energy consumption.
  • Customization: Custom designs ensure that the mixers can be optimized for specific processes, whether you are mixing high-viscosity liquids, fine powders, or gas-liquid mixtures.

The Neutrone® In-Line Static Mixers is a versatile and efficient solution for a wide range of mixing applications. With custom designs and materials, these mixers are ideal for industries that require precise and consistent mixing of liquids, solids, and gases. The radial mixing and flow division ensure uniform results, while the lack of moving parts reduces energy consumption and maintenance. Whether you're in chemical processing, food production, water treatment, or oil refining, Neutrone® In-Liner Static Mixers offer a reliable, cost-effective, and low-maintenance solution.
