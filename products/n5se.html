N5SE Series

Neutrone® Side Entry Mixers (N5SE Series) are designed to provide reliable, efficient, and safe mixing solutions for large-scale industrial applications. These gear-driven mixers are equipped with a shut-off device for added safety and ease of operation especially useful in situations where top-entry mixers would be inefficient or impractical, offering a range of benefits and key features. Here’s a detailed overview:

Key Features of the Neutrone® N5SE Series Side Entry Mixers:

Gear-Driven Design

The N5SE Series utilizes a gear-driven system, providing strong, consistent mixing performance. This design ensures durability and low maintenance, making it ideal for demanding industrial processes.

Shut-Off Device for Safety

A shut-off device is included in these mixers, enhancing safety during operation. It allows the mixer to be easily disconnected for maintenance or when it’s not in use, preventing accidental operation and ensuring greater safety for personnel.

Space-Saving and Practical Design

These side-entry mixers are mounted on the side of the tank, making them ideal for applications with limited headroom or restricted access. Unlike top-entry mixers that require long shafts and more complex installation, side-entry mixers are more compact and easier to install in large tanks or vessels with confined spaces.

Cost-Effective Solution for Large Tanks

The Neutrone® N5SE Series is particularly well-suited for large tank installations. Side-entry mixers do not require the long, expensive shafts that are needed in top-entry designs for very large tanks, making them a more economical option for mixing in such environments.

Efficient and Uniform Mixing

Despite their compact design, side-entry mixers can provide powerful and uniform mixing, ensuring that the contents of the tank are thoroughly blended. This is especially critical in applications where consistent material flow or uniformity is required, such as in chemical processing, wastewater treatment, and other industrial applications.

Durable Construction

Built to withstand heavy-duty applications, these mixers are designed for long-lasting performance in harsh industrial environments. The robust gear-driven system ensures reliable operation with minimal downtime.

Benefits of Neutrone® N5SE Side Entry Mixers:

  • Space Efficiency: Side-entry mixers are ideal for installations where space is limited or where access from above (as required for top-entry mixers) is not possible.
  • High Efficiency: These mixers provide consistent, high-efficiency mixing with minimal pressure drop. The radial mixing action ensures optimal blending of fluids and solids in a wide range of applications.
  • Lower Capital Investment: For large tank installations, side-entry mixers like the Neutrone® N5SE Series can be more affordable compared to top-entry mixers due to simpler installation and fewer mechanical components.
  • Improved Safety: The built-in shut-off device offers an added layer of protection, ensuring operators can easily isolate the mixer during maintenance or when it's not in use, minimizing the risk of accidents.
  • Cost-Efficiency: The N5SE Series offers a cost-effective solution for large tank mixing, providing high efficiency without the need for complex shaft systems, which are typically required in tall or deep tanks.

Neutrone® N5SE Series Side Entry Mixers are a reliable, safe, and efficient solution for industrial mixing applications, particularly in large tank installations. Their gear-driven design, space-saving configuration, and safety features make them an ideal choice for industries such as chemical processing, wastewater treatment, food production, and oil refining. Whether you need to handle high-viscosity fluids, large-scale blending, or operate in confined spaces, the Neutrone® N5SE Series offers a robust and cost-effective alternative to traditional top-entry mixing systems.
