NHSM Series

Neutrone®'s high shear mixer, NHSM Series designs offer excellent flexibility and performance for a variety of industrial mixing processes. These mixers can be configured for permanent mounting directly to the mix tank or suspended over the vessel using either portable or fixed lifting mechanisms. This flexibility allows you to maximize efficiency by using the same mixer in multiple vessels or different areas of the plant.

Key Features and Benefits of the NHSM Series Mixers:

Flexible Mounting Options

Rotor-stator mixers offer flexible installation options, including permanent mounting for stable, fixed operations and portable lifting mechanisms for mobility across vessels and plant areas. Adjustable agitator positioning ensures precise mixing to suit various materials, optimizing efficiency and versatility in diverse applications.

Rotor/Stator Design

Rotor-stator mixers feature a four-blade rotor within a close-tolerance stator, generating high shear forces for intense mixing. The rotor's high-speed motion drives materials through the stator, creating shear and turbulence. This design ensures efficient particle size reduction, emulsification, and effective mixing for demanding applications.

Versatile Mixing Applications

High shear rotor-stator mixers are versatile tools for particle size reduction, homogenization, and emulsification. They ensure uniformity by breaking down particles, solids, and immiscible liquids like oil and water into stable, consistent mixtures. Widely used in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics, they enhance efficiency and product quality.

High Energy Performance

Designed to rotate at speeds that produce energy levels often equal to, or better than, a conventional homogenizer. This means the mixer can handle demanding applications that require precise and intense mixing, while also offering energy-efficient operation.

Benefits of Neutrone® High Shear Mixers, NHSM Series:

  • Energy Efficiency: The high-energy rotor/stator action achieves quick and effective mixing, which often results in energy savings when compared to traditional homogenizers.
  • Versatility: The portable lifting mechanism option allows you to use the mixer in multiple vessels or locations within your plant, increasing its versatility and utility across various processes.
  • Customization: The ability to adjust the position of the mixer within the vessel enables fine-tuning of the mixing process, ensuring that a wide variety of materials can be handled efficiently.
  • Fast Processing: The high shear rate ensures that mixing tasks like particle size reduction, emulsification, and homogenization can be completed quickly and consistently, improving throughput and product quality.
  • Improved Product Consistency: By achieving rapid and uniform mixing, these mixers ensure that the final product has consistent texture, stability, and uniformity, which is essential for high-quality output in industries like pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and food production.

Neutrone® N5SE Series Side Entry Mixers are a reliable, safe, and efficient solution for industrial mixing applications, particularly in large tank installations. Their gear-driven design, space-saving configuration, and safety features make them an ideal choice for industries such as chemical processing, wastewater treatment, food production, and oil refining. Whether you need to handle high-viscosity fluids, large-scale blending, or operate in confined spaces, the Neutrone® N5SE Series offers a robust and cost-effective alternative to traditional top-entry mixing systems.
