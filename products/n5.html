N5 Series

Neutrone® Bottom Entry Mixers, (N5BE Serie)s are specifically designed for applications that require high flow efficiency and powerful mixing performance in industrial processes. These mixers are ideal for applications that demand consistent homogenization, blending, and solid suspension, as well as applications where heat transfer efficiency is critical. Here's an overview of the key features and applications of the N5BE Series:

Key Features of Neutrone® Bottom Entry Mixers, N5BE Series:

High Flow Efficiency

Engineered to provide high flow efficiency, ensuring that mixing is achieved quickly and uniformly. This helps meet various process requirements such as blending components, preventing stratification, and promoting effective heat transfer across large volumes.

Homogenization

Designed to prevent stratification in liquids. Stratification occurs when different liquid layers form due to differences in density or temperature. By ensuring thorough mixing, these bottom-entry mixers keep the mixture homogeneous, preventing the formation of unwanted layers, which is crucial in processes like chemical mixing and fermentation.

Blending Components

Ideal for blending different components of varying viscosities or densities. Whether you're mixing powders into liquids, combining different liquids, or blending slurries, the N5BE Series offers the flexibility and power to achieve uniformity without excessive shear or damage to sensitive components.

Solid Suspension

Excel at suspending solids in liquids, ensuring that solid particles remain evenly distributed throughout the mixture. This is especially important in applications where sedimentation or settling of solids would be problematic, such as in wastewater treatment, food processing, and chemical manufacturing.

Heat Transfer

In applications that require heat transfer, such as in reactors or large mixing tanks, the N5BE Series ensures uniform temperature distribution. By promoting efficient mixing, these mixers help optimize heat exchange, prevent hot spots, and ensure consistent thermal conditions throughout the system.

Key Benefits:

  • Improved Mixing Efficiency: High flow efficiency ensures that mixing processes are completed faster, reducing operational time and energy costs.
  • High Efficiency: These mixers provide consistent, high-efficiency mixing with minimal pressure drop. The radial mixing action ensures optimal blending of fluids and solids in a wide range of applications.
  • Uniform Mixtures: With superior blending capabilities, these mixers ensure that materials are consistently and thoroughly mixed, which is essential for high-quality production and process stability.
  • Prevents Stratification: The N5BE Series helps prevent unwanted stratification or layering in large tanks or reactors, ensuring a uniform product and reducing the risk of inconsistent performance.
  • Solid Suspension and Prevention of Settling: Particularly beneficial in applications where solids need to stay suspended in liquids, such as in slurry or wastewater treatment.
  • Efficient Heat Transfer: By ensuring even mixing, the N5BE Series promotes uniform heat distribution, enhancing the effectiveness of heat exchange and improving overall process efficiency.

The Neutrone® Bottom Entry Mixers, N5BE Series, offer an efficient solution for a wide range of industrial mixing needs. Whether you are looking to homogenize liquids, blend components, suspend solids, or optimize heat transfer, these mixers provide reliable, cost-effective, and high-performance solutions for large-scale industrial operations. Their high flow efficiency and robust design make them an ideal choice for demanding applications in chemical processing, pharmaceuticals, food processing, wastewater treatment, and more.
