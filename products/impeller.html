Impeller Series

Neutrone®’s range of impellers is designed to optimize mixing performance, reduce energy consumption, and lower both capital and operating costs across various industrial applications. The right impeller is crucial for achieving efficient mixing, and Neutrone® offers a modern selection that caters to different mixing needs, from high-efficiency flow to specialized applications like solid-liquid, liquid-liquid, and liquid-gas dispersion.

Neutrone® Impeller Range Overview:

  • NE-3 (High Efficiency Impeller):

    Application: Suitable for axial flow mixing.

    Features: The NE-3 impeller is designed to generate high flow efficiency while consuming lower power compared to traditional axial flow impellers. This makes it an excellent choice for energy-efficient processes that require high throughput with minimal energy use.

    Benefit: Reduced operating costs due to energy savings and improved performance efficiency in various liquid mixing applications.

  • NB-4/NB-3 (Traditional Impeller):

    Application: Ideal for larger volume applications, especially solid-liquid drawing processes.

    Features: The NB-4/NB-3 impeller provides high shear and robust mixing capabilities for handling large volumes of fluids with solids. It is a traditional design often used in industries like mining, chemical processing, and wastewater treatment.

    Benefit: This impeller is reliable for larger tank sizes and processes requiring solid-liquid dispersion and homogenization..

  • NM-3 (Advanced Marine Propeller):

    Application: Optimized for liquid-liquid mixing processes.

    Features: The NM-3 impeller is a marine-style propeller designed for applications where the mixing of two or more immiscible liquids is required. It generates a smooth, uniform mixing action ideal for processes that need efficient liquid-liquid dispersion without excessive shear.

    Benefit: Ideal for industries like pharmaceuticals, chemical blending, and food processing, where precise and gentle mixing of liquids is crucial.

  • NR-6C (Rushton Turbine):

    Application: Designed for liquid-gas dispersion, such as in aeration and fermentation processes.

    Features: The NR-6C is a Rushton turbine impeller, widely used in gas-liquid dispersion applications like bioreactors, fermenters, and aeration tanks. Its design is particularly suited for introducing air or gas into liquids, aiding in the dissolution of gases or enhancing microbial activity.

    Benefit: High gas dispersion efficiency with minimal energy input, making it ideal for applications in wastewater treatment, biotechnology, and chemical manufacturing.

  • NSX (High Shear Impeller):

    Application: Best for high shear processes where intensive mixing is required.

    Features: The NSX impeller is designed to generate high flow and intense shear forces, making it suitable for applications involving viscous liquids, solids dispersion, or emulsification. It can handle challenging mixing tasks like high-viscosity fluids or fine dispersion of powders into liquids.

    Benefit: It is highly effective for industries such as paint and coatings, pharmaceutical manufacturing, and food processing, where efficient emulsification or dispersion of high-shear materials is critical.

Key Benefits of Neutrone Impellers:

  • Improved Mixing Process Perfomance: Each impeller is designed to optimize specific mixing needs, from high-efficiency axial mixing to high shear and dispersion for complex processes. This ensures consistent product quality and improved operational efficiency.
  • Lower Capital and Operating Costs: Neutrone impellers are engineered to reduce power consumption and wear, leading to lower energy costs and reduced maintenance needs. This results in overall cost savings throughout the life cycle of the mixing equipment.
  • Customizable to Application Mode: The wide range of impeller types allows you to select the ideal solution for your specific application, whether it's solid-liquid mixing, liquid-liquid dispersion, or liquid-gas dispersion. This versatility ensures that your mixing process is tailored for maximum efficiency and performance.

Neutrone®'s impeller range offers highly efficient, cost-effective solutions for various industrial mixing needs. Whether you are dealing with high-flow liquid mixing, solid-liquid dispersion, or gas-liquid interactions, Neutrone® impellers provide optimal performance, reduced energy consumption, and cost savings. Their customizable designs and advanced engineering ensure that Neutrone impellers are well-suited for a wide variety of industries, including chemical processing, food production, biotechnology, and wastewater treatment.
